$$\Large {\displaystyle g(x)=\frac{5}{9}x^{3} + \frac{5}{3}x^{2} + \frac{1}{9}x - \frac{2}{3}\pi} $$

What is the value of:¶

$$ \Large {\frac {d}{dx}\, \left( f(x)\cdot g(x) \right) }\ \ \mathrm{at} {\ \ x\, =\, 3\pi} $$

  • Don't use decimal approximations for the rational numbers.
  • I want an exact answer, not a decimal approximation.
In [8]:
function_f = 3 * sp.sin(x / 3) * sp.cos(3 * x)
In [10]:
function_g = sp.Rational(5,9) * (x ** 3) + sp.Rational(5,3) * (x ** 2) + sp.Rational(1, 9) * x - sp.Rational(2,3) * sp.pi
In [13]:
sp.diff(function_f * function_g).subs({x: 3 * sp.pi})
Out[13]:
$\displaystyle - \frac{\pi}{3} + 15 \pi^{2} + 15 \pi^{3}$
